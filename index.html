Why swim with the Redwoods?

Mercer Island Redwoods is a group of adult swimmers affiliated with United States Masters Swimming. We take both our swimming and our fun seriously.

We emphasize fitness, conditioning, and community — and we have a competitive streak, too. We support the swimming goals of all our members. People of all skill levels and all adult ages are always welcome to drop in, sample the experience, or jump in as regulars. Your first swim is on us.

Our group has been together at the Mercerwood Shore Club pool since the mid 1990s, but dates back to the 1980s. You’ll find swimmers in their early 20s through their 80s — and everywhere in between.

What it costs to swim

Choose the option that fits your schedule. Quarters start in January, April, July, and October.

General

  • $380 per quarter
  • $140 per month
  • $14 drop-in

Young Masters (35 and younger)

  • $280 per quarter
  • $110 per month
  • $11 drop-in

MSC Members

  • $190 per quarter
  • $70 per month
  • $14 drop-in

We accept cash, personal check (payable to Mercer Island Redwoods), or Venmo (@Thomas-Grandine) (4302). Zelle and PayPal are also available — contact for details.

Daily rhythm

Weekday workouts, bright and early

We meet Monday through Friday from 5:30am to 7:00am. Expect a mix of distance, IM, sprint, and the occasional surprise set. Guests are always welcome — drop in for $14 and leave payment with the coach on deck.

Guests and members can sign up for coaching slots to keep mornings fresh.
